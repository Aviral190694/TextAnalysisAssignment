
International Guest House
International Guest House which is Situated  on the University Road and opposite the Faculty of Music, the International Guest House (IGH) is barely one hundred metres from the Vishvidyalaya  Metro Station and one hundred metres from “the Ridge” (Kamla Nehru Park). Facilities include two committee rooms, the one on the ground floor offering state of the art facilities. The IGH has wifi, safe and clean surroundings and is managed by professional and helpful staff who attend to our guests at all hours.
The IGH is a favorite with academic guests of the University from India and abroad. 
All bookings are made through the departments/centres/faculties of the University.
